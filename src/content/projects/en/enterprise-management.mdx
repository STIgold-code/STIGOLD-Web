## The Challenge

The client managed their operations using spreadsheets and manual processes, leading to frequent errors and wasted time.

## The Solution

We developed a comprehensive web platform that centralizes inventory management, billing, and reporting into a single system.

### Key Features

- Real-time metrics dashboard
- Inventory module with low stock alerts
- Integrated electronic billing
- Customizable reports with PDF/Excel export

## Results

- **60%** reduction in management time
- **3 branches** connected on a single platform
- **0 errors** in billing since implementation
